Site Plan for Gastromoz Restaurant

Site Name

Gastromoz Restaurant

This name was selected to reflect the premier culinary destination in Mozambique, celebrating the rich and diverse flavors of Mozambican cuisine while incorporating modern culinary trends.

Optional domain availability: gastromoz.com

Site Purpose

The website for Gastromoz serves as a digital gateway to showcase the restaurant's offerings, celebrate Mozambique's rich culinary heritage, and engage with a global audience.

Scenarios

  • What are the diverse flavors of Mozambican cuisine?
  • How can I make an order?

Color Schema

Primary Color: darkgrey - Used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: firebrick - Used for background highlights and buttons.

Typography

Primary Font: Roboto - Used for body text.

Secondary Font: Anton - Used for headings.
